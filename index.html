About Me


Welcome to my page! My pronouns are he/him/his. As a young kid I wanted to work in either medicine or technology but after high school I pursued healtcare and earned my B.S. in Cell & Molecular Biology. The past two years I've been working as a certified nursing assistant in a hospital gaining patient care experience for medical school. During the time, I learned a lot about myself and healthcare however around Christmas 2020 a thought crossed my mind, "There are aspects of being a healtcare professional I really do not enjoy". After a bit of soul-searching and researching I switched to my backup plan, technology. That's how I ended up here! Web Development is completely new to me and I have zero coding experience apart from the prework. I'm looking forward to getting to know everyone and learning as much as I can to jump into an exciting and rewarding career in web development!
